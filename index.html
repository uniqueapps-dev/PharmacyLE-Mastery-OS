How to Win
This system is engineered to make exam success inevitable.
Not by hoping you feel motivated — but by removing the need for it.

Why This System Exists
Most exam candidates fail not from lack of knowledge — but from lack of execution. They study when they feel like it, avoid their weak areas, and lose momentum after missed days.
THE CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLE
This system removes decision-making. You do not decide what to study. The system assigns it. You execute. The system adapts. Repeat until exam day.
📋
How to Use the App Daily
1
Open the app — check Mission tab
Your 4-task mission is pre-assigned. No planning needed. Read your "What Next" card — it tells you exactly what to do first.
2
Complete the Study Block (45–60 min)
Open Library → Curriculum. Study the assigned topic. Use active recall — close the material and test yourself on what you remember.
3
Do the MCQ Block (Practice tab)
Tap Practice → Daily Quiz → Start Now. 20 questions, timed. Read every explanation — even for correct answers. This is where learning happens.
4
Revision Block
Review the second assigned topic using a different approach: write out key points from memory, then check what you missed.
5
Weak Area Drill
Go to Practice → By Topic. Find your red-marked weak area. Drill 10 questions. The system selects these automatically based on your performance data.
6
Tick off each task on Mission tab
Mark tasks complete as you finish them. When all 4 are done, your streak increases. The system logs your session and adjusts tomorrow's mission.
⚙️
How the System Adapts to You
Weak Area Reinforcement
Every time you answer a question, the system records your accuracy per topic. Any topic below 60% is flagged as WEAK and automatically prioritised in future missions. Weak areas repeat daily until you reach 70%+.
Spaced Repetition (SRS)
Questions you answer incorrectly are added to your SRS queue and resurfaced at scientifically optimised intervals — 1 day, then 3 days, then longer. Go to Intel tab to start SRS sessions when items are due.
Adaptive Topic Selection
The Mission Engine reads your accuracy data daily. Weak topics get promoted into your mission. Strong topics rotate out. High-weight exam topics (those worth the most marks) always take priority.
AI Question Generation
When connected, use Practice → AI Generate to create unlimited custom MCQs on any drug, topic, or clinical scenario. Save them to your question bank for offline use.
⚡
What Happens If You Miss Days
Missing a day is not failure. The system detects it and activates Catch-Up Mode automatically.
CATCH-UP MODE
The system rebalances your workload — redistributing missed content without overloading a single day. Your streak resets to 1, but your performance data is preserved. The mission adapts immediately.
⚠️ The only unrecoverable mistake is quitting. Missing days slows progress but never ends it. Return, execute today's mission, and trust the compound effect.
🗺️
Your 5 Tools — What Each Tab Does
🕐
Mission — Daily Execution Engine
Your command centre. Shows today's 4 tasks, progress ring, streak, exam countdown, and the "What Next" instruction. Start here every day. Do not navigate away until you have your first task in hand.
✅
Practice — MCQs & Testing
Five modes: Daily Quiz (20Q mixed), By Topic (drill any subject), Timed Session (40Q with pressure), Mock Exam (120Q full simulation), and AI Generate (unlimited custom MCQs via OpenRouter). This is where your performance data is built.
🎭
OSCE — Simulation Training
Patient scenarios with a live countdown timer and marking rubric. Practice counselling, clinical emergencies, ethics, and calculations. Used for PEBC Qualifying Exam and PSI. Tap a scenario, start the timer, tick your rubric items as you go, and receive structured feedback at the end.
📈
Intel — Performance & Weakness Map
Your performance dashboard. Shows overall accuracy, 7-day trend chart, all topics with accuracy bars (red = weak, green = strong), and your Spaced Repetition queue. Check this weekly. Red bars are your highest-priority targets.
📚
Library — Materials, Audio & Settings
Four sub-tabs: Resources (curated references with priority ratings), Audio (tap-to-play summaries that work offline), Curriculum (6-month study plan + full syllabus), and Settings (exam target, API key, speech rate, data reset).
